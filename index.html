Alpine mountain created in World Machine

Cool visual effect created in Unity along with the new Post Processing Stack

Terrain created in World Machine and imported into MapMagic as a heightmap with a bitmap for textures. Used MapMagic to place trees, objects, and grass, and to create the beach shoreline. Manually edited in the road and bridge. The best effect here is the lighting which I highly modified based on research. For example, there is next to zero ambient lighting. It's 99% direct lighing from a single source turned extremely high but with linear color space and filmic color grading to remove the typical whiteout.
